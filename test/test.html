TimeLens Verification

This page contains various timestamp formats to test the TimeLens extension.

1. ISO 8601 Formats

Standard ISO: 2026-02-19T12:00:00Z

With Milliseconds: 2026-02-19T14:30:00.123Z

In sentence: The server restarted at 2026-02-19T08:15:00Z unexpectedly.

2. Explicit UTC/GMT Formats

Full UTC: 2026-05-20 16:00:00 UTC

Full GMT: 2026-05-20 16:00:00 GMT

Short Month UTC: Feb 19 18:45 UTC

Short Month GMT: Feb 19 18:45:30 GMT

ISO with Offset: 2026-02-19T12:00:00+05:30

In sentence: Event starts at May 01 09:00:00 UTC sharp.

3. Implicit UTC (No Timezone)

Short: 2026-01-01 00:00

With Seconds: 2026-01-01 00:00:30
